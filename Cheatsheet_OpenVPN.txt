[+] Connect to OpenVPN with preconfigured username/password:

[-] Step 1 - 
Create a text file with two lines, first line containing your username and second line containing your password. 
Save this file, for eg., as up.txt.

[-] Step 2 - 
Create a file named, for eg., connect.sh and save it to the same folder as up.txt from Step 1.
and add the following line to it - 

openvpn --config YourOpenVPNConnectionFile.ovpn --auth-user-pass up.txt

[-] Step 3 - 
After the Step 1 and 2 are done. Every time you need to connect to your VPN, just open a terminal, and type the following command - 

sh connect.sh

And Kudos, your connection would be established.
